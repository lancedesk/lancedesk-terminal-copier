# Support and SLA

## Community support (default)

- GitHub Issues: best-effort responses.
- No guaranteed response or resolution times.

## Sponsored support (planned)

The project roadmap includes optional paid/sponsored support tiers for teams needing faster turnaround.

Indicative targets for sponsored support:

- Initial response: within 1 business day.
- High-severity guidance: same business day when feasible.
- Priority bug triage and coordinated release communication.

Contact: `support@lancedesk.com`
