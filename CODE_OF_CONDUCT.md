# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions.
- Assume good intent and give actionable feedback.
- Focus on ideas and behavior, not personal attacks.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Personal insults, intimidation, or doxxing.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after warnings.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or other contributions that violate this policy.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to: `conduct@lancedesk.com`.
